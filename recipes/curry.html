Sweet Curry

Japanese Potato Sweet Curry

This recipe covers how to make a Sweet Thai Chicken Coconut Curry. Curry is a very customizable item to make, so many different ingredients can be added or omitted depending on preference.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Garlic
  • Fresh ginger
  • Canned coconut milk
  • Shredded carrots
  • Thai red curry past
  • Lime juice
  • Brown sugar
  • Fresh spinach

Steps

  1. Pre cook the chicken breasts in the oven
  2. In a large pot, add garlic and oil
  3. Slightly brown the garlic in the oil
  4. Once browned, add 2 cans of coconut milk
  5. Add chicken, ginger, carrots, brown sugar, and red curry paste to the pot
  6. Let this all simmer for 20 minutes, and then add the spinach and lime juice
  7. Simmer for 3 more minutes and then take off heat
