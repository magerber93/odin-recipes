Baked Chicken Breast

This recipe covers how to make oven baked chicken breasts. This is a quick and easy recipe that can feed an entire family in under an hour. It is highly customizable depending on preferences and allergies.

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Kosher Salt
  • Preferred seasonings

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F
  2. Prepare and clean the chicken breasts however preferred
  3. Melt butter and coat breasts on both sides
  4. Season breasts with seasoning of choice
  5. Allow breasts to sit and marinate with seasonings for 10 minutes
  6. Place in oven and allow to cook for 15-20 minutes or an internal temperature of 165F
