Roasted Brussel Sprouts with Garlic

This recipe covers how to make oven roasted brussel sprouts with garlic. This is a very easy dish to prepare and make, and goes with almost any meat.

Ingredients

  • 1lb brussel sprouts
  • 4-6 Tbs extra virgin olive oil
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 Tbs balsamic vinegar

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400F and trim the bottoms off of the brussel sprouts
  2. Place olive oil, garlic, vinegar, and brussel sprouts in a pan on the stove until slightly brown on the bottom
  3. Transfer the sprouts to the oven for 10-20 minutes, shaking every 5 minutes
  4. Taste and add more preferred seasonings if needed
